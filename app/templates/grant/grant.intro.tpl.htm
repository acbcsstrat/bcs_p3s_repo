Grant and Publishing Fees

Here you can respond to an intention to grant which we can submit to complete the grant process. You'll be asked a small number of questions, but we get all the remaining information for you from the published data. We'll also calculate all of the fees that need to be paid with the submission. The action is then available to either review or check out.

The French and German translations of this application are required to be uploaded.
